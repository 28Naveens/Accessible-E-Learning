About Our Accessibility Features

Text-to-Speech (TTS)

Text-to-Speech (TTS) functionality allows users to input text and have it read aloud to them. This is helpful for students with visual impairments or those who prefer auditory learning.

Sign Language

Sign language videos are available for key terms and phrases. These videos help students who are hearing impaired understand content visually, in a language they are familiar with.

Subtitles

Subtitles are provided for all videos to support students who are hearing impaired. The subtitles will display text to match the content spoken or presented in videos.
